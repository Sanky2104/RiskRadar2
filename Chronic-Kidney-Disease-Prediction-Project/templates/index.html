Chronic Kidney Disease Prediction



Specific Gravity Measures urine concentration to assess kidney function.


Hyper Tension Indicates if the patient has high blood pressure (Yes = 1, No = 0).


Hemoglobin The level of hemoglobin in blood, measured in grams (gms).


Diabetes Mellitus Indicates if the patient has diabetes (Yes = 1, No = 0).


Albumin The level of albumin in urine, a key kidney function indicator (0-5 scale).


Appetite Indicates the patient's appetite status (Good = 1, Poor = 0).


Red Blood Cell Count The number of red blood cells in blood, measured in millions per cubic millimeter (Millions/cmm).


Pus Cell Indicates the condition of pus cells in urine (Normal = 0, Abnormal = 1).
