Texture Mean The variation in texture (contrast) of cells observed in the tissue sample.


Smoothness Mean Measures the smoothness of cell boundaries in the sample.


Compactness Mean How tightly the cell components are packed together.


Symmetry Mean The symmetry of cells in the tissue, compared to a perfect circular shape.


Fractal Dimension Mean A mathematical measure of the complexity of the cell edges.


Texture SE Standard error of the variation in texture (contrast) in the sample.


Smoothness SE Standard error of cell boundary smoothness in the sample.


Symmetry SE Standard error of the symmetry values across cells in the sample.


Symmetry Worst The worst observed value for symmetry in the sample.
